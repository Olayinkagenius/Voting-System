  • About Us

    Who are we and what do we do.

    Who we are?

    DesignIT Africa is a gen Z tech org whose keen interest lies in equipping teenagers with digital skills needed to thrive in the 21st century. These teenagers grow further in life to become change agents in their communities utilizing the skills they learn from our organization.

    Discover More

    What we do?

    We organize annual digital skills training sessions where we recruit up to 20 scholars. These scholars undergo 2 months intense training on coding, graphic designing and digital marketing skills. Our classes are coupled with leadership and entrepreneurial skills so that our scholars can broaden their perspectives and think about how to utilize their tech skills for societal impact.

    Other things we do are: tech awareness tours, tech summits, and hackathons.
