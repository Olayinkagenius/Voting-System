Who can vote?

Of course you have a right to vote

Of course we all have the right to vote but this site is for the students of the Faculty of Science to choose their leaders for the 2021/22 session.

Your votes are not counted if you are not verified as a student of any of the 9 departments in the Faculty of Science. In the hot spirit of aluta, the faculty of science hope you get through a smooth and stressless voting process.

Register

Create an account and verify as a student of the Faculty Of Science to get the chance to vote.

Sign In

Login to your account using the Email Address you registered with (Your Student Mail) and your unique password.

Vote

Remember, your vote matters. Choose wisely. Somewhere inside us resides the power to change the world. Let it out.
